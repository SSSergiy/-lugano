About Lugano Business Forum

During our two-day world-class business conference, we gather more than business leaders. Join us to improve your leadership skills, network with fellow C-level professionals, and create a memorable event that lasts!

AS it was Before

Discover the Power of collaboration at the Lugano Business Forum

  • 2 Days of the forum
  • 100+ Speakers
  • 30+ Topics for discussion
  • 1000+ Participants

The Lugano Business Forum 2024 is an exceptional gathering of entrepreneurs, industry leaders, policymakers, and innovators from around the world.

This prestigious event aims to create an engaging platform for collaborative discussions, networking, and knowledge-sharing, focusing on unlocking opportunities for global growth.

Participants will have the chance to explore emerging trends, engage in insightful conversations, and build strategic partnerships that will drive economic growth and innovation on a global scale.
